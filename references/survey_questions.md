## Scoring Reference

### Likert Scale Interpretation
| Mean Score | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 1.00 – 1.79 | Very Low |
| 1.80 – 2.59 | Low |
| 2.60 – 3.39 | Moderate |
| 3.40 – 4.19 | High |
| 4.20 – 5.00 | Very High |

Acceptable threshold: **Mean ≥ 3.50**

### SUS Score Interpretation
| SUS Score | Grade | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| ≥ 90 | A | Excellent |
| 80 – 89 | B | Good |
| 68 – 79 | C | Above Average |
| 51 – 67 | D | Below Average |
| < 51 | F | Poor |

SUS formula: `((sum of odd items - 5) + (25 - sum of even items)) * 2.5`

Acceptable threshold: **SUS score ≥ 68**
